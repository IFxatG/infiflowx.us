# **App Name**: Cash Home Buyers Landing Page

## Core Features:

- Lead Capture Form: A simple form to collect user's full name, email address, phone number, and property address.
- Fast Offer Generation: Backend processing of submitted property data to generate a fair cash offer. LLM as a tool for helping in market research to formulate initial bid, factoring local price variations.
- Single-Page Design: A streamlined, single-page layout focusing on the lead capture form and benefits for motivated sellers.
- Mobile Optimization: Ensuring the landing page is fully responsive and optimized for fast loading on mobile devices.
- Trust Signals: Integration of social proof elements such as star ratings and customer testimonials.

## Style Guidelines:

- Main Background: Off-white or light beige (approachable).
- Headers/Navigation: Deep Navy Blue (trustworthy/stable).
- CTA Button: Bright Orange or Vibrant Red (urgent/action-oriented).
- Success Icons: Green (symbolizing a fresh start/cash).
- Body font: 'PT Sans' sans-serif font, ensures readability and clarity.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' serif, for elegant and high-end look.
- Use simple and recognizable icons to represent benefits and features.
- Clean, uncluttered layout with a focus on the lead capture form and clear benefit-driven headlines.
- Subtle animations on button hover or form submission to improve user experience.
